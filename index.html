What makes Wonderland Montessori so popular with families?

Learning tailored to the individual

Each child is given lessons at the right time when they are ready to learn.

Education for the whole child

Along with academic skills, social, emotional, and physical development are emphasized.

Choice empowers

Independence and self-confidence are fostered by the freedom to move and choose activities.

Research-based

Montessori education is consistently supported by neurology and human development research.

Develop a lifelong love of learning

Wonderland Montessori schools offer authentic Montessori education to children from infancy (nido) through elementary school, with professionally trained guides certified by the Association Montessori Internationale (AMI) or the American Montessori Society (AMS) at training centers throughout the world. Throughout the formative years of development, Wonderland Montessori nurtures children academically, socially and emotionally.
